A New Kind of Learning Space

Home education, reimagined.

Wonder is a co-learning space built for home-educated children. Home education gives your child freedom, depth and real personalisation — Wonder gives them everything else: a beautiful space, expert guides, a like-minded community and real-world adventures. You keep the flexibility. We handle the rest. Drop them off. Pick them up. Everything in between is extraordinary.

For Children

Home-educated children get the freedom that school can't offer. Wonder adds what home can't always provide — peers, expert guides, hands-on tools and a space that sparks curiosity every single day.

For Parents

Home educating doesn't mean giving up your day. Drop your child at Wonder and get back to work, errands or exercise. We give home-educating families the flexibility they chose, without the guilt.

For the Future

Home education is already ahead of the curve. Wonder accelerates it — with AI-first projects, outdoor adventures and a growing network of clubs your child can visit anywhere. More clubs, more friends, more experiences.

Our Philosophy

A place where children come alive

Wonder is Wimbledon's home education co-learning space. We bring together home-educated children in a beautiful, purposeful environment — with expert guides, a 1:5 ratio and a full programme of hands-on learning and outdoor adventure. Your child gets the community and resources of a great school. You keep every advantage of home education.

No Screens Under 7

Young children thrive through touch, movement and real-world experience. We keep technology meaningful and age-appropriate.

AI-First Courses

For older learners we introduce AI as a creative thinking tool — building skills that genuinely matter tomorrow.

Everything Included

All materials, resources and teacher training are covered. Just show up and wonder.

Government Apprenticeship Partners

Through a government apprenticeship programme, we guarantee our 1:5 teacher-child ratio in every session.

Our Commitment

Never more than 1 to 5

1:5

Every teacher looks after a maximum of 5 children. This applies to every indoor session and every outdoor adventure, every single day. No exceptions.

Health & Wellness First

Spaces designed for flourishing minds and bodies

Our indoor environments are as intentional as our outdoor ones. Every Wonder space is thoughtfully curated around children's physical and mental health.

💧

Humidifiers

Optimal air moisture supports respiratory health and concentration — especially important for young children spending extended time indoors.

🌬️

Air Purifiers

HEPA-filtered air purifiers run continuously, removing allergens, pollutants and particulates for the cleanest possible indoor air.

🌿

Living Greenery

Real plants throughout our spaces — proven to reduce stress, improve air quality and create a calming atmosphere that helps children focus.

☀️

Outdoor Priority

Whenever weather allows, learning moves outside. Fresh air, daylight and movement are core to our daily rhythm — not optional extras.

🧀

Mindfulness & Movement

Regular yoga, breathwork and mindfulness woven into every Wonder day — building emotional regulation and calm from the very start.

🍎

Whole-Child Approach

We consider the whole child — physical, emotional and intellectual. Our programme supports parents with wellness guidance beyond the Wonder session.

is our classroom

We partner with local parks, Merton Council and Wimbledon's heritage sites — turning every outing into an unforgettable lesson. All outdoor visits maintain our guaranteed 1:5 ratio.

Pocket Park Planting

At Wimbledon Village's Pocket Park, children plant seeds, learn about ecosystems and watch life grow — hands in the soil, minds fully engaged.

Wimbledon Village

The Last Windmill

Wimbledon Common

Yoga on the Common

Wimbledon Common

Composting & Growing

Pottery Studio

Foraging Walks

Wonder Giving

Connecting generations.
Rewarding every contribution.

Wonder is more than education — it is a community built on the belief that everyone has something extraordinary to give. Through Wonder Giving, we create meaningful, paid opportunities for people with Down's syndrome and older members of our community to play an active role in shaping the next generation.

Down's Syndrome Programme

Real roles. Real rewards.

People with Down's syndrome bring warmth, patience and a unique ability to connect with children that is genuinely irreplaceable. We create structured, supported roles within our clubs — from learning assistants to creative workshop leads. Every hour earns Wonder Rewards: credits redeemable for experiences, sessions and goods.

Elders Programme

Wisdom shared. Life enriched.

A lifetime of experience is one of the most valuable things a child can encounter. Wonder Giving connects retired professionals, grandparents and older community members with Wonder clubs — as storytellers, craft guides, history keepers and mentors. Every hour earns Wonder Rewards.
